Jember Fashion Carnival (JFC) 2019 is an annual fashion carnival held in the beautiful city of Jember in East Java, Indonesia taking place on the 4th of August 2019. This carnival shows off extravagant costumes that emphasize the traditional Indonesian motif. This 18th year of JFC, the main theme that is chosen is Tribal Grandeur and over 6000 carnival guests are participating.

The idea of JFC itself started off by the idea of Dynand Fariz, a local fashion designer and educator.

It was originally his carnival and first held in 2001. It was then officially named as JFC on the first of January 2003. Since then, the carnival has been held every year in the month of August. Sadly, Fariz passed away earlier this year in April but has left us with a wonderful event to his legacy.
